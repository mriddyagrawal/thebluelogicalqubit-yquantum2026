The Challenge

Given a quantum circuit as a QASM file, find the peak bitstring — the most probable measurement outcome.

Why is this hard?
A 50-qubit statevector needs 250 ≈ 1.1 quadrillion complex amplitudes. Direct simulation is impossible beyond ~30 qubits.

Scaling (P1 – P9)

Our Approach

1. Load QASM circuit
↓
2. Optimize (Qiskit + Tket + PyZX)
↓
3. Analyze structure (factoring, cancellations)
↓
4. Simulate (MPS / Exact Statevector)
↓
5. Extract peak bitstring
